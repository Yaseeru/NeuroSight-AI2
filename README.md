# NeuroSight AI

AI-Powered Alzheimer's Screening Tool

## Overview

NeuroSight AI is a web application that provides instant AI-generated assessments of MRI brain scans for early Alzheimer's detection. Upload your MRI scan and receive rapid screening results across 4 stages of Alzheimer's disease.

## Classification Stages

The AI classifies MRI scans into one of four stages:
- Non-Demented
- Very Mild Dementia
- Mild Dementia
- Moderate Dementia
